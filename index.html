Elan and ηvision

THE GRANDEST TECHO-CULTURAL FIESTA
IN AP & TELANGANA
Elan and ηVision is the annual techno-cultural fest of IIT Hyderabad and is one of the largest fests in South India. Elan refers to the cultural part and ηVision cites the technological part of fest. Elan & ηVision boasts of several professional and semi-professional crowd pulling events and memorable pro-shows. It promises to be a grand event that exhibits the best of cultural performances, technical solutions, and advances from the student community. Students hail from various institutes throughout the country and take away bagful memories and experiences.

About Us

This fest is held in the campus of IIT Hyderabad and is completely organised by the students. Right after the fest gets completed, preparations for the next year’s fest begin. Students of IITH amalgamate into a team and put all their efforts into organising this fest. The whole organising team is divided into 19 domains. Each domain has its own motive and functionality. All the domains coordinate with each other and host this fabulous fest.
